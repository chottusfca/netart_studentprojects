Who Are We?

Riverside Drive Charter School's mission is to help our diverse student population cultivate the curiosity and skills to be life long learners. Our STArt (Science, Technology, and Art) curriculum provides hands on, interdisciplinary learning that is the key to developing the critical thinking, creativity, collaboration, and communication proficiencies that are important in the 21st Century. We are committed to creating and maintaining a thriving community of educators, staff, parents, and community partners who work together to prepare students for the challenges of college and careers.
