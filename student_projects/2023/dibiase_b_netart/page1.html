I'm Too Young To Be Relating To This

The title, “4 Morant” indicates that she's stuck in the past feeling an overwhelming sense of guilt for believing she could have been happy and in love. She had wanted to live and move on from her breakup and all her past mistakes but is now stuck living in the past and unable to heal. She’s lost all motivation for change, falling deeper and deeper into her depressive state. She sees it as “it is what it is” saying “better luck next time” as to mean in another life maybe she could start over, however she’s too far gone. Doja can no longer truly live, and is now forced to relive the same hurtful and traumatic moments of her life over and over again, hence the “I’ve been moving in backward directions during astral projection” and “don’t mess with the past tense” lines.
